I'm Nick Larsen. I currently work as a data engineer at Stack Overflow. I'm also finishing up a masters of computer science at Georgia Tech. In my free time, I do interviews for Karat. I also live stream on Twitch. I'm a husband, a father, a high power rocketry enthusiast and I love games.

My passion is building tools that make people's lives easier and educating people to be able to achieve their goals on their own. The pit of success is a metaphor for tools that lead you down the path of success simply by using those tools. This is what I strive for when building things, and this site is where I share those tools.
